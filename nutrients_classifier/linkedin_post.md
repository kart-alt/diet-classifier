🚀 I am incredibly excited to announce the launch of my latest project: **NutriVision AI**! 🌿

NutriVision AI is a full-stack, AI-powered dietary tracking platform that I built to bridge the gap between computer vision and daily health tracking. 

I set out to build an application that completely eliminates the friction of manual calorie counting, and I’m thrilled to share what’s under the hood!

🛠️ **Key Technical Features:**
*   **Computer Vision Engine:** Upload a photo of your meal and the custom-trained CNN paired with YOLOv8n instantly identifies the food and portions it out.
*   **PWA Barcode Scanning:** Built a mobile-friendly barcode scanner in React that decodes UPCs and queries the global OpenFoodFacts API in real-time.
*   **Live Data Syncing:** Integrated a lightning-fast Pandas search algorithm against a 7,000+ item USDA dataset, all securely logged to a multi-user SQLite database via FastAPI.
*   **Context-Aware AI Chatbot:** Integrated Google's blazing-fast **Gemini 2.5 Flash** LLM directly into the UI. The chatbot reads your real-time SQLite database logs and provides conversational, hyper-personalized dietary advice!
*   **DevOps & Deployment:** Fully containerized the entire stack (React, Python, TensorFlow) using Docker and Docker Compose.

I learned so much about decoupling complex AI pipelines from responsive frontend architectures during this build.
